WeatherMann Application

Have you ever been sitting at home studying on a rainy day and wished you had the perfect playlist? WeatherMann is here to match all your weather vibes. Think a Walkman for the weather! Upon visiting the page, type in the city of your choosing. This search will then result in displaying the current weather for your city as well as playing a songlist of warmer or winter tunes to coincide with the outdoor temperatures. This site will also locally store your searches, so that they persist when returning to the page. If at any point you would like to remove the search history, simply select the "Clear History" button and your page will reset.

My Role:
Created two functionalities for the app. The first was setting conditions for one of our functionalities, which was generating the Spotify embed by inserting <iframe> player for a playlist based on the weather/temperature of the location.
Then next functionalility was creating the function that generated an image of the location/city to set as the background. This was done by calling the Unsplash API, which allowed me to fetch the link to the full image url of the photo, and then set the "background-image" in CSS.
